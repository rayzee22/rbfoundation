About The Founder:


DR JIMOH RAUF BADAMOSI, FOUNDER OF RAUF BADAMOSI FOUNDATION With a heart full of compassion and a soul driven by kindness, Dr. Jimoh Rauf Badamosi embarked on a legendary journey of philanthropy, leaving an indelible mark on humanity. The Rauf Badamosi Foundation (RBF), a brainchild of his altruism, stands as a testament to his unwavering commitment to uplifting the less fortunate. This not-for-profit charity organization is a beacon of hope, shining bright with a passion that permeates every facet of its existence. The RBF's raison d'être is to elevate the economic and material well-being of the less-privileged peopled and underserved communities in Nigeria, offering a lifeline to those struggling to stay afloat. Instituted in loving memory of his late father, Alhaji Rauf Badamosi, the RBF serves as a poignant tribute to a legacy of love and generosity. Through his foundation, Dr. Jimoh Rauf Badamosi continues to weave a tapestry of kindness, thread by thread, transforming lives and communities forever.

2. Board of Trustees:

A Board of Trustees (BOT), comprising of select men and women unquestionable integrity and character, administers the activities of the Foundation. The BOT is principally responsible for the decision on where and how the funds of the foundation would be applied. They are also responsible for selecting, interviewing and investigating prospective recipients of the Foundation’s assistance.
